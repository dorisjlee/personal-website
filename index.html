Doris Jung Lin Lee

I am a physics and astrophysics undergrad from UC Berkeley. My current research interest lies in human computer interaction (citizen science, crowdsourcing, visualization) and data mining (for scientific applications). I have worked on fabrication technology accessible to Makers and designers, mining large cosmological datasets, and supercomputer simulations of astrophysical objects. I am interested in creating technology that bridges the knowledge and skillsets of different communities of people. I will be moving to UIUC to begin my graduate studies in CS this August.
